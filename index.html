What We Offer

At Selvanayaki Groups, we go beyond supplying equipment. Our technical proficiency covers every aspect of fluid management and electrical mechanics, from factory-floor installations to massive government water infrastructure projects.

Industrial Installations

Expert erection, alignment, and commissioning of heavy-duty pumps and electrical motors for factories and manufacturing plants.

Motor Rewinding & Overhauls

Complete restoration, coil rewinding, and mechanical overhauling to bring your aging or burnt motors back to factory-standard performance.

Government Contracts
