The Total Water Solution Across All Sectors

Agricultural Sector

End-to-end irrigation solutions designed to maximize crop yield and ensure highly efficient water usage across your farmlands.

  • Pumps: High-discharge Borewell Submersibles (KSB / Kirloskar)
  • Piping: PVC Pipes,Borewell Pipes (Turebore)
  • Support: Heavy-duty Ropes & Nets (Tufropes)
  • Motors: Bharat Bijlee Agricultural Motors

Industrial Sector

Robust, heavy-duty fluid management systems built for continuous operations in factories, mills, and processing plants.

  • Pumps: Chemical, Process & Surface Pumps (Ksb pumps, Kirloskar)
  • Valves: High-Pressure Industrial Valves (Ksb valves)
  • Piping: PVC Pipes, SWR pipes,cPVC,uPVS (Turebore)
  • Support: Safety Ropes & Nets (Tufropes)
  • Motors: Energy-Efficient Bharat Bijlee Electric Motors

Domestic & Residential

Reliable water supply and pressure boosting solutions ensuring consistent water flow for homes and residential complexes.

  • Pumps: Openwell & Monoblock Surface Pumps (Ksb pumps, Kirloskar)
  • Systems: Pressure Boosting Hydropneumatic Systems
  • Piping: FOAMCORE Pipes & PVC Pipes (Turebore)
  • Service: Installation & Genuine Spares

Commercial & Building Services

High-capacity systems engineered for hotels, hospitals, and corporate parks, ensuring uninterrupted water management and fire safety.

  • Pumps: Inline & End Suction Pumps (Ksb pumps, Kirloskar)
  • Safety: FM Approved Fire Fighting Pump Sets (Ksb pumps, Kirloskar)
  • HVAC: Chilled Water Circulation Pumps (Ksb pumps, Kirloskar)
  • Piping: PVC,uPVC,cPVC Pipes (Turebore)

Municipal & Water Treatment

Large-scale infrastructure solutions for city water supply distribution, sewage handling, and wastewater treatment plants.

  • Pumps: Heavy-Duty Sewage & Drainage Submersibles (Ksb pumps, Kirloskar)
  • Treatment: STP & ETP Pumping Solutions (Ksb pumps, Kirloskar)
  • Valves: Large Diameter Sluice & Butterfly Valves (Ksb pumps)
  • Piping: PVC, FOAMCORE, SWR Pipes (Turebore)
