Who We Are

Decades of Trust & Excellence

Trusted Partner for Pumps, Motors, Industrial Ropes & Piping Solutions Sinces 1996, Selvanayaki Groups has been a reliable name in the industrial sector, providing top-quality products and services to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Located in the heart of Siddhapudur, Coimbatore, Selvanayaki Electricals has established itself as the region's premier destination for high-quality industrial and agricultural equipment.

We don't just sell products; we engineer solutions. As an authorized dealer and certified service center for industry giants like KSB, Kirloskar, Bharat Bijlee, Tufropes and Turebore, we ensure that your operations run seamlessly with zero downtime.
